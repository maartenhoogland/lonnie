What it is about / check it out!

HEC SEED is the first international entrepreneurship forum held at HEC Paris.

Get inspired by our 5 high-profile guest speakers, choose from 18 interactive workshops tailored to your needs, and discover over 30 outstanding startups on the entrepreneurship floor.

We bring together the brightest students, young founders & succesful startups to foster international networking experiences.
