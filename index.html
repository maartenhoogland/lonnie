Event FAQS / find your answers

Ask a question
  • When and where does the event take place?

    • The event takes place at HEC Paris, a university campus situated in the peaceful town of Jouy En Josas in the southern suburbs of Paris. We start registrations at the reception desk from 08:00 onwards with the first event slated for 09:30. We will start serving breakfast as well to get you warmed up.

  • Yes. Food. What about food?

    • We shall be serving breakfast between 08:00 and 09:30 where you can start your day with some piping hot croissants and pains au chocolat along with fruits and beverages. We break for lunch at 12:15 when you can refuel with some delicious food and beverages. And finally, we round off the event with dinner and drinks at 20:00. Apart from all the meals, we have free coffee and other beverages throughout the day.

  • That’s great! Is there a party at the end?

    • We believe in the Work Hard and Party Harder spirit. Of course we have dinner lined up along with drinks for purchase over the counter for you to enjoy networking and unwind from your long day.

  • Cool! So how do I get there?

    • To get there, you have 3 main options:

    The HEC SEED Shuttle (RECOMMENDED) – We will have a small shuttle or a few cars waiting for you from Massy Palaiseau (on RER B line when coming from Paris) to HEC Paris Campus between 8am to 9am (We are still planning it out. More information will be intimated closer to the event). But please contact us asap if you are interested.

    Train – Jouy En Josas is on RER C line and Massy Palaiseau (on RER B line when coming from Paris) is the closest train hub from Paris. Please consult www.transilien.com for any train related information. After you reach Jouy En Station train station, you can take a 5 minute bus ride to the campus from there.

    Car – We have ample (free!) parking space for you here on campus and driving down is pretty convenient. Google Maps/Waze will do a better job than us at navigating. Alternatively, you can always take cabs from Paris. Just mention the exact address (HEC Paris, 1 rue de la Liberation, Jouy En Josas) to the driver and you should be alright.

    Of course, you can always fly down here in your personal helicopter. Just let us know in advance and we shall have our helipad ready to receive you.

  • How do I get back to Paris?

    • We have tied up with our good friends over at Heetch, who will be providing each one of you with 10 Euros off coupon for your cab ride back into Paris. You can obviously still take the train.

  • Cool! So what’s the program for the day?

    • You can find the full schedule on our website. Alternatively, you can click here.You will obviously get a more complete itinerary closer to the event. Kindly note that we are still adding new speakers and workshops as we speak. Hence, you should follow us on one of the social media platforms right now!

  • I heard that there are more than 15 workshops and a lot of speeches. Can I attend them all?

    • Unless you can be at two places at once, it is not practically possible to attend all the workshops. You will have to choose among different workshops since there will be multiple workshops running (at different places) during a single round. However, you can still attend all the speeches and other events since they won’t be overlapping.

  • How do I choose the exact workshops that I want to attend?

    • If you have already bought the tickets, you will receive an email from us a few days before the event to send us your preference list on workshops. Based on demand, we will block the events for you.

  • Do I need to get anything? And what about my jacket/luggage?

    • Please make sure you have a copy of your ticket on your phone along with an ID card to get in. Apart from that, it’s completely up to you. We have a lot of interactive sessions planned out, so maybe something to scribble on may come in handy. Also, don’t forget that startups will be in recruiting mode as well, so a CV can be a useful weapon of choice. We have a cloak room for you throughout the event for your jackets and other luggage so that you can peacefully enjoy the event.

  • Is there a dress code?

    • Whatever makes you comfortable, we say! But we might have to stop you at the door if you decide on a giraffe costume though.

  • Is there Wi-Fi?

    • Yes, you can enjoy complimentary Wi-Fi throughout the event.

  • What languages will be spoken at the event?

    • Although the event will be completely in English, we can speak over 12 languages within our team. Rest assured, we will always be there to help you out.

  • Sounds really awesome! I’m in. How do I get my tickets?

    • You can buy the tickets online from over here. Online ticket sales close on 16th April, 2015. Due to Vigipirate – the French national security system – we are not legally allowed to sell tickets to non-HEC students at the door. (Do contact us directly if you want to buy the tickets after the deadline and we can figure out if it's still possible on a case by case basis).

  • 25 Euros! That’s a lot! What do I get for my money?

    • Apart from the inspiring speakers, interactive workshops and networking opportunities, you also have all 3 meals included in the ticket price. Further, we shall be providing you will free shuttle services in the morning from Massy Palaiseau to the event site at HEC Campus and a 10 Euro Heetch off coupon for your cab back. Basically we have you covered from the start of the event till the networking party at the end.

  • I’m coming from out of town. Can you help me with accommodation?

    • Although we do not have anything confirmed yet, we are working hard to get some accommodation arranged for some of you who may be interested. Do watch this space for more updates or contact us directly.

  • Anything else?

    • Well that’s it for now! We hope you have already bought your tickets. If not, what are you waiting for!? In case you have any other queries, please do not hesitate to get in touch with us through our social media channels or the contact form below. We love hearing from you guys. Hope you enjoy the event and don’t forget to follow us on our official social pages over at Facebook or Twitter to stay tuned with our latest updates.
